About Protowave

This is an educational tool designed to help visualize how common digital communication protocols transmit data. The waveforms are simplified representations to illustrate the core concepts of each protocol. For instance, I2C ACK/NACK bits are assumed as ACK, and PCIe is a highly conceptual view of its physical layer signaling.
